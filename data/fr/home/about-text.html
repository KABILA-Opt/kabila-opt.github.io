Our aim is to optimize the valuable combination of gastronomy, experience, and exceeding customer satisfaction.

Being passionate about cooking since an early age, I used to help my mom in the kitchen after doing grocery shopping in the free market daily.

Seeking the path to fulfill my culinary dream, I obtained a professional degree in gastronomy cooking.

The love of cooking allows me to be creative and be inspired by new recipies. This has lead to experimentation with new flavours from around the world, including French and Moroccan cuisines.

Kabila Bistro offers an authentic,traditional and inspired menu using fresh and local ingredients.
